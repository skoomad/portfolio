Blender Projects

Geometry Nodes Animation

My cleanest project in Blender. First I found a model on Mecabricks that I liked, then in Blender I broke it down to pieces, such as foundation, walls, dooors and etc. Then I set up a geometry nodes and moved everything up, animating how it falls. Starting with foundation and then going part by part until the roof is settled.

Software used: Mecabricks, Blender, DaVinci Resolve

Pippy Longstocking

My first animation project, where I got a rigged model of Pippy and her horse. I wanted to do something silly with her movemement and decided to go for a timestop, using Dio's ZA WARUDO SFX. Animating her preparing to jump and freeze mid air, by rotating camera was fun and fruitful. A small animation done quickly.

Software used: Blender

Lego Movie

My biggest project in Blender which I struggled with the most. Having troubles with exporting files to Unity and later finding out the models I made in Mecabricks had overlapping LEGO parts causing visual issues. And animations being a bit janky due to dyssynchronization between Blender and Unity. But overall I learned a lot about animating in Blender, rigging and making sure to check things twice. It was a great experience and I am happy for it.

Software used: Mecabricks, Blender, Unity
